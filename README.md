# 📄 Administrative Automation: Legal Compliance & Document Generation Framework

## 🚀 Project Overview
Designed and engineered a custom GPT framework focused on document automation within highly regulated frameworks (Credit Reporting Agencies and consumer compliance). This personal project showcases the ability to train AI on complex regulatory standards to automate high-stakes correspondence and administrative dispute workflows.

*   **The Problem:** Navigating bureaucratic agency demands requires meticulous adherence to formatting, specific terminology, and statutory compliance.
*   **The Automation:** Built a system that ingests raw case facts and outputs perfectly structured, legally aligned correspondence letters.

---

## ⚙️ Logic & Prompt Architecture
*This repository details the operational strategy behind automating specialized administrative paperwork.*

### 1. Regulatory Context Conditioning
*   Programmed the AI with strict guardrails based on consumer compliance guidelines (e.g., Fair Credit Reporting Act principles) to ensure every generated letter hit necessary statutory markers.

### 2. Variable Capture & Document Synthesis
*   The GPT was engineered to execute a strict "slot-filling" conversational script to gather specific transaction details, dates, and account identifiers before drafting the document, minimizing errors.

### 3. Tone & Friction Engineering
*   Conditioned the model's output tone to be firm, professional, and formal—mirroring the exact diction required to pass automated agency sorting and reach human review.

## 🛠️ Core Capabilities Demonstrated
*   **Regulatory Analysis:** Translating complex guidelines into operational AI rules.
*   **Operations Optimization:** Reducing document preparation time from hours to minutes.
*   **Data Integrity:** Structuring inputs to ensure absolute accuracy in high-stakes output.
